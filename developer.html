FAQ

    Is LifeScan API connection available outside the US?

    Yes, LifeScan API connection is accessible in all markets where the OneTouch Reveal™ mobile app is available.

    How do I request a LifeScan connection?

    Just fill out the form at the bottom of our home page and we'll be in touch shortly to discuss your request.

    Once my request is approved, how long does the credentialling process take?

    Our goal is to get you set up quickly. A Non-Disclosure Agreement is required as is a connectivity agreement. The time it takes to complete these depends largely on your organization and the time it takes to review and sign these documents.

    What are LifeScan’s specs for connection with API?

    Just fill out the form at the bottom of our home page and we'll be in touch shortly to discuss your request.

    Can apps made with the LifeScan API access my data without consent?

    The LifeScan API enables developers to connect their apps with OneTouch Reveal™ data, but it does not automatically grant those apps access to OneTouch® user data. Within an app, developers implement a mechanism for users to connect the user’s OneTouch Reveal™ account to the app, which requires user authentication and HIPAA authorization (via OAuth 2.0) for data access to occur.

    At LifeScan we are focused on maintaining data privacy. You can update your connections at any time within the OneTouch Reveal™ mobile app or web application.
